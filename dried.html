Dried food

Drying is a good way of preserving food like fruits, vegetables, herbs and meat. Most types of foods can be dried by using an oven, with a dehydrator or in the sun. Drying is also a way for you to save money, not wasting food by worrying so much about the expiration date. Dry food can be healthier, especially fruit that has a greater concentration of fiber and antioxidants than fresh fruits.
